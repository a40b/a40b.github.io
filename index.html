We accelerate the identification, the capture and building of a structured, scalable and manageable growth of your international potential and presence



We are retained and project based advisors who will help you. Our goal is to support you to generate profitable results and enhance enterprise value from overseas expansion



We guide the strategic consideration of why and how to best structure your internatinal presence and we oversee the implementation of sustainable operating plans, across indutry sectors and markets

Our Services

Scope

Whether embarking on overseas expansion, wanting to move into additional markets or needing to restructure under-performing areas of an existing international presence, we bring expertise and reinforce your resources. We guide your team to focus on the fundamentals of extending your reach and diagnose gaps in competencies and capacity to attain your objectives. Our aim is to assist you to move smarter.

Resource

We are accomplished in market analysis, partner selection, contract agreements, establishing distribution channels, creating actionable plans and networking for sustained, profitable expansion. We will guide the transition to culturally adapted marketing programs and the development of an effective commercial approach.

Our experience is worldwide, across most classes of trade, especially in the consumer and industrial B to B sectors. It encompasses all disciplines for advancing business and the assessment of market entry options, including both legal and pricing structures.

Results

We take the long term view of exporting: to prioritize viable opportunities, finding or supporting overseas partner's that respect the value of collaboration in representing your brand, while strengthening your organizational capacity to maintain vibrant international operations and sales.

Additional service

We also promote the entry and growth of new enterprise into the US market. We can assist in optimizing your supply chain and presenting alternatives for overseas manufacturing and licensing.

who we Are

Tony Patterson

As an independent business advisor active between Europe and the US, Tony brings a diverse experience across international business strategy, planning, execution and operational structures. This has spanned industries and markets with hands-on operational market building and value generation.

Moved into International Sales and Marketing with Mitsubishi Corporation European trading HQ in London, engaged in market and sector research, project initiation, financing and execution covering Europe, Middle East, Africa and Japan. Joined Business International in Rome (management consulting, merged into the Economist Group) advising major corporations on trading, investment conditions, regulations and strategies across Africa and Europe.

After starting in market research, planning and marketing with the trading group Mitsubishi Corporation EMEA region in London, he joined Business International in Rome (management consulting, now the Economist Group) advising major corporations on trading, investment conditions, regulations and strategies across Africa and Europe.

This was followed by corporate, marketing and senior regional management roles with Philip Morris International based in Switzerland, Nigeria, Ivory Coast, Senegal, Egypt and Dubai, scaling teams, nurturing partners and entering new markets.

He relocated to head up the UK publicly listed Adam & Harvey Group, engaged in diversified trading and distribution, with operations in Asia, Africa, Europe and the US covering, among others, industrial equipment, timber, steel, coach manufacture and textiles.

He moved to the USA in 2007 and joined Iredale Mineral Cosmetics as Chief Operating/Strategy Officer with US manufacturing and multi-channel sales in the US and international markets through distributors. More recently he was CEO with the premium gift and beauty brand Crabtree & Evelyn in the US, including the global manufacturing facility, ecommerce fulfillment and marketing functions, with some 200 suppliers, exporting globally and sold through a mix of branded stores, distributors and third party channels.

Tony is a Fellow of the UK Institute of Directors and lives between the US and Europe. He grew up in England, Scotland, Malta, Cyprus, Gibraltar, and Libya, and graduated in Economics in London.

Patricio Forno

Most recently, Patricio was with a leading US manufacturer, marketer and distributor of branded cosmetics with multichannel distribution in the US and 29 international markets. Tasked with the challenge of developing additional markets, the share of international sales grew from 15 to over 30%, expanding to 50 countries. This included extensive work in Mexico, across Latin America and Asia, especially Brazil, Singapore and S. Korea, Pan-European pricing strategies and new brand launches in Scandinavia.

This sustained export sales growth earned the company the 2016 US Presidential “E” Award, the highest recognition for significant contribution to US exports. He is a member of the District Export Council of Massachusetts (DEC), appointed under the U.S. Secretary of Commerce.

He studied Industrial Engineering at the Universidad de Chile and attended Business School at the Universidad Diego Portales, in his native Santiago. His Chilean professional life included founding and running a successful entrepreneurial venture selling fire prevention equipment, executive management of the largest wallcovering importer from Europe and Canada, distributing 15,000 products and running the operations of a leading wholesale and retail construction materials and equipment chain with an extensive supply chain.

After naturalizing in the US, he directed the sales and operations of a home furnishings manufacturer and then ran his own financial trading enterprise for 5 years, developing proprietary data management tools and bases to deliver fast, practical insight into financial trading.

Patricio lives in Connecticut and is an avid practitioner of mountaineering and – with his wife – of paragliding and motor biking. He is also an accomplished wilderness guide in Patagonia.
