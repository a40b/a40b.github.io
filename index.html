We accelerate the identification, the capture and building of a structured, scalable and manageable growth of your international potential and presence



We guide the strategic consideration of why and how to best develop your international presence and we oversee the implementation of sustainable operating plans, across indutry sectors and markets



We are retained and project based advisors. Our goal is to support you to generate profitable results and enhance enterprise value from overseas expansion

WHAT WE DO

Scope

Whether embarking on overseas expansion, diversification, considering new markets or needing to restructure under-performing areas of an existing international presence, we bring expertise to reinforce your resources.

We guide leadership and focus your team on the fundamentals of extending your reach. We will diagnose gaps in competencies and capacity across the footprint to attain your objectives and grow potential.

Our aim is to assist smaller to mid-size corporations, or divisions, to move smarter within the resource allocations supported by the business and partners. We take a hard look at the options. We look at the brand, service and product lines, the positioning, marketing and channels to understand how they need to be tailored for new markets.

We will spot the financial and other resource constraints and help you incorporate strategies and plans into the organization to address these.

We are accomplished in actionable market analysis, partner selection, contract agreements, establishing distribution channels, pricing and legal structures, creating support functions and assessing market entry options. We will guide the transition to culturally adapted marketing programs and the development of an effective commercial approach.

Outcomes

We take the long term view of international expansion: to prioritize viable opportunities, finding or supporting overseas partners who respect the value of collaboration in representing your brand.

We accelerate the capture of incremental volumes, margins or strategic advantage.

We transfer competency to your organization to maintain vibrant international operations and sales.

The goal is to generate enhanced enterprise value in a way that makes international business a compelling and integral part of your business.

who we Are

Tony Patterson

Tony has held leading international executive positions and corporate advisory roles with both Fortune 500 groups and smaller corporations. This has been across all regions from Europe to Asia, building strategies and operations to enter and develop international markets. He combines the practical in-market operational experience with the demands of strategic and financial planning behind exceptional results.

His leadership roles with companies such as Mitsubishi Corporation and Philip Morris International are complimented by the commercial smarts, discipline and flexibility needed in smaller businesses to operate internationally. This expertise has been used in industries spanning branded consumer goods, raw materials, transport and industrial equipment, new brand launches in mature markets to opening up consumers and customers in new markets.

After starting in market research, planning and marketing with the trading group Mitsubishi Corporation EMEA region in London, he joined Business International in Rome (management consulting, now the Economist Group) advising major corporations on trading, investment conditions, regulations and strategies across Africa and Europe.

He has lead and built initiatives encompassing direct trading, sales and distribution structures, import and export initiatives, manufacturing and licensee operations, both own affiliate and with 3rd party government and private structures.

Tony is a Fellow of the UK Institute of Directors and lives between the US and Europe. He graduated in Economics from London, grew up in England, Scotland, Malta, Cyprus, Gibraltar and Libya and has been based in Switzerland, Italy, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Nigeria, Egypt, Dubai, the USA and the UK.

Patricio Forno

Most recently, Patricio had the challenge to develop the international presence of Iredale Mineral Cosmetics, a leading manufacturer, marketer and distributor of branded cosmetics with multichannel distribution in the US and 29 overseas markets. The footprint was expanded to 50 countries and the share of international sales grew from 15% to over 30%. This included extensive work in Mexico and Brazil, across Latin America and Asia, especially Singapore and S. Korea, Pan-European pricing strategies and new brand launch in Scandinavia.

This sustained export sales growth earned the company the 2016 US Presidential “E” Award, the highest recognition for significant contribution to US exports. Patricio is a member of the District Export Council of Massachusetts (DEC), appointed under the U.S. Secretary of Commerce.

He studied Industrial Engineering at the Universidad de Chile and attended Business School at the Universidad Diego Portales, in his native Santiago. His Chilean professional life included founding a successful entrepreneurial venture selling fire prevention equipment, executive management of the largest wallcovering importer, distributing 15,000 products from Europe and Canada, and managing the operations and extensive supply chain of a leading wholesale and retail construction materials and equipment group.

After naturalizing in the US, he directed the sales and operations of a home furnishings manufacturer and then ran his own financial trading enterprise for 5 years, developing proprietary data base management tools to deliver fast, practical insight into financial trading.

A lifetime motorcyclist and trained wilderness guide in Patagonia. He enjoys mountaineering and lives in Connecticut where he and his wife are avid paragliders.
