Tony Patterson Strategic, Value Driven - International Business Expansion

    Tony brings a diverse experience across international business strategy, planning, execution and operational structures. This has spanned industries and markets with hands-on operational market building and value generation. After starting in market research, planning and marketing with the trading group Mitsubishi Corporation EMEA region in London, he joined Business International in Rome (management consulting, now the Economist Group) advising major corporations on trading, investment conditions, regulations and strategies across Africa and Europe. This was followed by corporate, marketing and senior regional management roles with Philip Morris International based in Switzerland, Nigeria, Ivory Coast, Senegal, Egypt and Dubai, scaling teams, nurturing partners and entering new markets. He relocated to head up the UK publicly listed Adam & Harvey Group, engaged in diversified trading and distribution, with operations in Asia, Africa, Europe and the US covering, among others, industrial equipment, timber, steel, coach manufacture and textiles. He moved to the USA in 2007 and joined Iredale Mineral Cosmetics as Chief Operating/Strategy Officer with US manufacturing and multi-channel sales in the US and international markets through distributors. More recently he was CEO with the premium gift and beauty brand Crabtree & Evelyn in the US, including the global manufacturing facility, ecommerce fulfillment and marketing functions, with some 200 suppliers, exporting globally and sold through a mix of branded stores, distributors and third party channels.
